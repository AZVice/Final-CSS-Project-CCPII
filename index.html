What is light pollution?

Light pollution is the human-made alteration of outdoor light levels from those occuring naturally.

What are the effects?

Light pollution disrupts wildife, impacts human health, wastes money and energy, contributions to climate change and blocks of view fo the universe.

What can be done about light pollution?

Following responsible lighting practices, passing dark sky friendy legislation, and advancing scentific research in this field are just some of the ways light pollution can be solved. You can get involved too!
